Her definition, and I will give it to you plainly, is that the New Jim Code is the use of new technologies that reflect and reproduce existing inequities, while being promoted and perceived as more objective or progressive than the discriminatory systems of an earlier era. The harm is old. The technology is new. And the sense that it is neutral and modern is exactly what lets it keep working, because a system that everyone believes is objective is a system that no one thinks to question.

Coded exposure, the uneven way systems make some people hyper-visible and others invisible. And technological benevolence, the trap of a tool that is sold as helping while it deepens the harm.
